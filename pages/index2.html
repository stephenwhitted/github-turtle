Dive Into the World of Tortoises

  • Tortoises are land-dwelling reptiles with lifespans that can exceed 100 years in some species.
  • They are known for their slow movement but strategic survival strategies that have stood the test of time.
  • Tortoises have a variety of shell shapes and sizes adapted to their environments, from arid deserts to lush forests.
  • They play key roles in their ecosystems, such as facilitating seed dispersal and maintaining healthy soil.
