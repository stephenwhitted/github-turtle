Sea turtles are one of the ocean's most ancient and fascinating creatures. They play crucial roles in marine ecosystems. Let's delve deeper into their world!

Learn About Sea Turtles

Sea turtles have been navigating the oceans for millions of years. They are vital for healthy seagrass beds and coral reefs, helping maintain the habitat's health and productivity. Unfortunately, they face numerous threats such as habitat destruction, accidental capture in fishing gear, and climate change.
